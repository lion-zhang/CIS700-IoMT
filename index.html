Introduction

This course is to explore selected topics in the Internet of Medical Things (IoMT). IoMT allows data from multiple medical devices to be aggregated and analyzed and actions of multiple devices to be synchronized and automated for patient monitoring and care. In principle, IoMT can be used to improve patient safety and treatment outcomes by leveraging diverse capabilities of individual devices to gain a more detailed and accurate picture of the evolving patient state and also by automating some of the tasks currently performed by caregivers. The course is to study how to develop such IoMT applications while assuring that they are safe, secure, and effective. Topics to be addressed are clinical decision support, wearable healthcare technologies, LLM in healthcare, efficacy and safety evaluation of IoMT, virtual patients, security and privacy, bias and fairness. The course will also discuss real-world applications in mental health, ophthalmology, dermatology, mechanical ventilator weaning, and smart alarms.
